Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is recognized by its butter flavor and the inclusion of chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • salt
  • baking soda
  • chocolate chips
  • butter
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. melt butter 90% and whisk until fully melted
  2. once butter is melted, whisk in the sugar and then the vanilla extract and egg
  3. add the flour, baking soda, and salt and stir until combined(don't overmix)
  4. stir in chocolate chips and scoop the dough. Place about 2 inches apart on the baking sheet.
  5. bake until the cookies are set on the edges but slightly underdone in the middle, about 7-10 minutes(depending on temperature).
