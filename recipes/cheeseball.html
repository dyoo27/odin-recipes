Korean Cheeseball Recipe

Description

Korean cheese balls have a delicious texture with a cheesy melty inside and crispy outside. This dish is incredibly easy to make, and you can stock these cheese balls in the refrigerator for quick snacks.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Mozzarella cheese grated
  • Seasoning-garlic powder, salt, sugar
  • Hot water
  • Melted butter
  • Cheese powder
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. mix flour, sugar, butter, and salt in a bowl
  2. slowly add hot water and mix until nice thick texture
  3. take 25g each, knead the dough, and roll to make a round shape
  4. cover dough with a wet towel
  5. weigh mozzarella cheese about 6 g each and shape into round shape
  6. add mozzarella cheese inside each round dough and close the dough into a round shape again *repeat*
  7. In a pan fry, heat oil over medium heat. Keep the fire in low heat and fry the cheese balls for about 3 minutes
  8. After the balls are cooked, rest them on a cooling rack
