Tteokkbokki recipe

Description

Tteokbokki (떡볶이) is a popular Korean street food made of soft, chewy rice cakes cooked in a sweet and spicy sauce made of gochujang.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • fish cakes
  • rice cakes
  • soy sauce
  • korean red pepper powder
  • anchovy stock
  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds
  • gochujang

Steps

  1. Mix the gochujang, Korean red pepper powder, soy sauce, garlic, and sugar in a bowl
  2. add the anchovy stock packet and water to a large pan and bring it to a boil. Once it boils, remove the stock packet and add the red pepper paste.
  3. Once the tteokbokki sauce comes to a boil, add in the rice cakes, fish cakes, and any vegetables. Boil for about 5 minutes until soft and tender.
  4. Finally, add toasted sesame oil, scallions, sesame seeds.
